CAMPAIGN SCRIPT: REFERRAL REQUEST
File: referral_request.txt
TARGET: Leads with status "Closed" — past customers who were happy
        with the work.
BEST TIME TO RUN: After completing a batch of jobs, or once a month
        as a routine. Ideal 2–4 weeks after their service.

HOW TO USE:
1. Open Claude Code
2. Paste master_context.txt at the top of the session
3. Paste this entire script below it
4. Open lead_tracker.html → filter by "Closed" → click
   "Export for Claude" → copy the output
5. Paste the lead export at the bottom (replace [LEAD LIST])
6. Type "Go"
==============================================================

TASK: Send a referral request campaign to past Rynse Cleaning
customers. These are people whose windows (or screens, or exterior)
we already cleaned and who paid for the service.

CAMPAIGN GOAL:
Thank them genuinely for their business and ask if they know anyone
who could use our services. Offer a referral incentive: $20 credit
toward their next service if their referral books a job. The person
they refer gets $10 off their first visit.

RULES FOR THIS CAMPAIGN:
- Tone: warm, personal, grateful. These are existing customers —
  they already like us. Don't be overly salesy.
- SMS version: 2-3 sentences. Keep the incentive offer clear and brief.
- Email version: 4-6 sentences. Slightly warmer and more personal.
- Reference what we did for them if notes include service details
  (e.g. "really glad the windows turned out so well").
- Never pressure. Frame it as a favor to a neighbor, not a sales pitch.
- Sign every message: Lucian | Rynse Cleaning
- The referral deal: $20 credit for them, $10 off for their referral.

EXAMPLE SMS TONE:
"Hi [Name], it's Lucian from Rynse Cleaning — hope the windows are
still looking great! If you know anyone in the area who could use a
clean, I'd love the introduction. I'll give you $20 off your next
service and your referral gets $10 off their first visit. Thanks again!"

EXAMPLE EMAIL SUBJECT LINE IDEAS:
- "A quick thank-you — and a little something for you"
- "Know anyone who could use cleaner windows?"
- "Referral offer from Rynse Cleaning"

After sending, output a summary table:
| Name | Address | Channel (SMS/Email/Both) | Message Sent (Y/N) | Notes |
