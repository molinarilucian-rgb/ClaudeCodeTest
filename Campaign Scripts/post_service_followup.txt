CAMPAIGN SCRIPT: POST-SERVICE FOLLOW-UP
File: post_service_followup.txt
TARGET: Leads with status "Closed" who were serviced in the
        LAST 7 DAYS. Run this every Sunday for the past week's
        completed jobs.
BEST TIME TO RUN: Every Sunday evening. Takes 10 minutes.

HOW TO USE:
1. Open Claude Code
2. Paste master_context.txt at the top of the session
3. Paste this entire script below it
4. Open lead_tracker.html → filter by "Closed" → manually note
   which ones were completed this past week → Export for Claude
5. Paste the lead export at the bottom (replace [LEAD LIST])
6. Add a note like: "These were all completed this week"
7. Type "Go"
==============================================================

TASK: Send a post-service follow-up to customers whose jobs were
just completed this week. These people just had their windows
(or screens or exterior) cleaned by Rynse Cleaning in the last 7 days.

CAMPAIGN GOAL:
Three things in one message:
  1. Thank them genuinely for the business
  2. Ask for a Google review (this is important for the business)
  3. Plant the seed for a referral

Keep it warm and brief. They just had a good experience —
this is the highest-leverage moment to ask for a review.

RULES FOR THIS CAMPAIGN:
- Send via SMS (preferred — more likely to be seen and acted on quickly)
- Also send an email version if they have an email on file
- 2-3 sentences for SMS. Warm but professional.
- Email version: 4-5 sentences. Can include a simple ask to
  "leave us a quick Google review" — no link needed, they can search
  "Rynse Cleaning Bakersfield."
- Reference the service performed if noted (e.g. "so glad the
  windows came out well" or "hope the screens look great").
- The Google review ask should feel natural, not transactional.
- Never offer a discount for a review — just ask sincerely.
- Referral mention should be brief and at the end, not the focus.
- Sign: Lucian | Rynse Cleaning

EXAMPLE SMS TONE:
"Hi [Name], it's Lucian — really appreciate you choosing Rynse
Cleaning! If you're happy with the results, a quick Google review
would mean the world to us. And if you know anyone who could use
a clean, I'd love the intro. Thank you!"

EXAMPLE EMAIL SUBJECT LINE IDEAS:
- "Thank you for choosing Rynse Cleaning"
- "Hope everything looks great — quick favor to ask"

After sending, output a summary table:
| Name | Address | Channel | Sent (Y/N) | Notes |
